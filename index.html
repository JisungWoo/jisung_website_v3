About Me

Turning data complexity into clarity.

Download Resume

I'm a Data Engineer who builds reliable, scalable data systems — and increasingly, AI-powered ones. At Honeywell, I engineer transformation logic across a Snowflake Enterprise Data Warehouse spanning 9,000+ objects, manage government-scale datasets under strict compliance, and coordinate offshore teams to deliver against tight deadlines. My stack spans Snowflake, Informatica, Control-M, SQL Server, and Python.

What sets me apart is how I'm bridging traditional data engineering with AI. I led an initiative to automate column-level lineage across our entire EDW using Snowflake's native LLM functions and Claude Opus — achieving 80% accuracy on a task that would have taken months manually. I've prototyped internal data catalog tooling in React, and I'm currently designing a semantic layer and metadata governance framework for the enterprise warehouse.

My approach starts with the meaning behind the data, not just the mechanics of moving it. I care deeply about structure, documentation, and building solutions that others can understand, maintain, and trust. Whether it's enterprise pipelines or autonomous AI systems, the standard is the same: precision combined with thoughtful communication.

Outside of work, I'm building a multi-agent orchestration platform using OpenClaw and OpenAI Codex — a system where an orchestrator assigns tasks to specialized AI agents (developer, QA, research council) that autonomously build, test, and debate features, then notify me for human-in-the-loop validation. It's targeted at the mental health therapy space as a SaaS product.
